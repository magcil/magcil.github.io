Welcome to the Multimedia Analysis Group of the Computational Intelligence Lab (MagCIL) of the the Institute of Informatics and Telecommunications , of the National Center for Scientific Research "Demokritos". In MagCIL we are doing research in multimodal machine learning: multimodal speech analytics, music information analysis, video classification, speech recognition, speech emotion recognition, video summarization and image retrieval.
