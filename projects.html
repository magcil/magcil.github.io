DeployAI Development and Deployment of the European AI-on-demand Platform. Aims to design, deploy, and operate a fully functional AI-On-Demand platform (AIoDP) that promotes trustworthy, ethical, and transparent European AI solutions for industry and the public sector. 2024 2027
FaRADAI Frugal and Robust AI for Defense Advanced Intelligence. An EU-funded project focused on developing novel approaches, algorithms, and tools to strengthen the applicability and impact of AI in defence systems. 2022 2026
Museek Automatic Music Monitoring of Public Performance. Develops a lightweight, device-friendly system that can automatically monitor and recognize music played in public venues such as restaurants, hotels, cafes, clubs, or bars. The project is a collaboration between MagCIL and GEA, the official music copyright collective management organization of Greece. 2022 2024
SmartSubs SmartSubs builds a realtime framework that analyzes recorded speech from theatrical plays, in order to automatically stream subtitles to smartglasses used by people with hearing problems. 2021 2023
